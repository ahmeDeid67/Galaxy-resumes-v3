Frequently Asked Question

Q: Will my resume be made by a Human or AI?

A: Here, at Galaxy Resumes we believe that AI generates a generic content, nothing unique. Hence it’s content is deemed plagiarized and as such more than 90% of AI based resumes face rejections.

Hence, it’s Humans who develop resumes at Galaxy Resumes which are entirely customized based on experiences, skills, abilities etc. that brings the best of YOU! Your resume will also be infused with ATS optimized keywords and as a result will have a very high success rate.
